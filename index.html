ABOUT US

Who We Are

Lif is a compassionate community of mental health professionals committed to helping those in need. Our website aims to improve all aspects of life.

Our Story

  • Founded in 2025, Lif emerged from a shared vision among students who recognized the growing need for accessible, evidence-based mental health services.

  • We believe that mental health care should be personalized, compassionate, and rooted in the latest scientific research. Our revolutionary application works to provide comprehensive support tailored to each individual's unique needs.

  • Today, we serve thousands of individuals across the country, offering a range of services.

  • Our Mission

    To provide accessible, evidence-based mental health care that empowers individuals to achieve their goals.

  • Our Values

    Compassion, integrity, innovation, and inclusivity guide everything we do, ensuring every person feels heard, respected, and supported.
